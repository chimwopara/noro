Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 April 2026  ·  Last updated: 22 April 2026

Makeup Day Ltd ("Makeup Day", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect data when you use our platform at makeupday.ng and associated applications.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: full name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
  • Artist application data: years of experience, specialties, brands used, location area, kit details, Instagram handle, and referral source.
  • Booking preferences: skin tone preference (stored locally on your device), makeup style preferences, brand level preferences, and scheduling information.
  • Communications: messages you send us via email or the platform.

We also collect certain information automatically when you use the platform:

  • Device type, browser type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referral source.
  • Approximate location data (city level) derived from your IP address, used solely to suggest nearby artists.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To connect clients with makeup artists based on location, style, and brand preferences.
  • To send transactional emails (welcome, account verification, booking confirmations).
  • To review artist applications and manage the artist onboarding process.
  • To improve and personalise the Makeup Day experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Artists: when you make a booking, relevant booking details (name, contact, preferences) are shared with the assigned artist.
  • Service providers: third-party tools that help us operate the platform (e.g., email delivery, analytics) under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: where required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@makeupday.ng.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@makeupday.ng.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Makeup Day uses browser local storage to save your skin tone preference across sessions. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers unless you actively make a booking. We use minimal cookies for session management and analytics.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

Makeup Day is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by a prominent notice on the platform. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@makeupday.ng
